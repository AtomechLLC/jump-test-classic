1. Press Jump

Initial jump force is applied to the character as upward Y velocity

2. Gravity Takes Effect

Each frame, the character moves by their Y velocity, then gravity is subtracted from it

3. The Peak and Fall

Y velocity reaches 0 and reverses as gravity keeps pulling, accelerating into a fall
